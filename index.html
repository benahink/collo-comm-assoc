About Us

Who We Are

A Living Tradition We are Collo of the USA

Established in 2023, Collo Community Association is the strongest leadership organization in the U.S. Our mission is to raise the influence and well-being of the Collo community through connectivity, civic engagement, public awareness, and nonprofit support. We do this work in partnership with members all across the U.S. to provide a hub for cultural awareness.
